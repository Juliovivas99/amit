Retainer vs. Fixed Price

The initial 3-month retainer delivers a usable MVP — not every advanced capability in the production-ready V1 roadmap. The fixed-price option reflects a higher total investment because it contractually commits to a formally scoped production-ready V1, with defined deliverables, locked requirements, formal scope management, delivery accountability, and greater project risk assumed by the developer.

$24,000 initial retainer → communications-first usable MVP with unified workspace and three dashboards. $60,000–$80,000 fixed price → production-ready V1 with contractual delivery. MVP delivery: 8–12 weeks. Production-ready V1: 12–16 weeks.
